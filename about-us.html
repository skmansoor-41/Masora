About Masora

Masora is India's emerging career platform focused on empowering fresh graduates to land their dream IT jobs. We bridge the gap between opportunity and skill by delivering job alerts, mentorship resources, and career guidance — all under one roof.

Our mission is to make hiring transparent, inclusive, and accessible, especially for those without elite college tags. We support skill-first careers and promote growth beyond resumes.
