About Masora

Masora is a fresher-centric career platform dedicated to helping students and job seekers land their first IT job. We bridge the gap between fresh graduates and top tech companies by delivering timely job alerts, off-campus hiring updates, and essential interview preparation materials.

With a focus on B.Tech, BCA, MCA, and other IT-related backgrounds, Masora aims to make your job hunt faster, smarter, and more successful. Whether you're looking for WFH internships or walk-in drives, we’ve got you covered — daily.

Our mission is simple: Empower every fresher to build a confident and successful IT career through the right opportunities and resources.
