About Masora

Masora is a fresher-first platform focused on helping Indian graduates break into the IT world. We provide daily job alerts, curated off-campus drives, placement prep content, and access to real HR interview experiences – all free and accessible.

We believe in equal opportunity hiring, and our mission is to make career discovery and job access smoother for students and early-stage professionals.
